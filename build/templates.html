How to make templates for the pantorouter

A frequently asked question about the pantorouter is where to get tenon templates for it.

However, I do not know what size tenons you would like to make, which size router bit you want to use, and what size your follower bearing are. In addition, it actually makes very little sense to use paper templates when making tenon templates on the pantorouter. However, if your follower bearing is exactly twice the diameter of your router bit, then the template needs to be exactly twice the size of your tenon. (See how it works)
