Pantorouter plans DXF files

DXF files allow you to view the pantorouter CAD model in AutoCad, and some other programs also use DXF. I have DXF files available for the plans.

However, the DXF files are large and few people use them, so they are not part of the standard download Zip file for use off-line. You can however download the DXF files separately.

You are currently browsing the off-line copy of the plans on your computer.
However, if you click on "Browse plans on-line" from the download page (a link to that page was emailed to you), then click on "DXF CAD files" from the main index to download the DXF files separately.
